/ 02 Featured · Plastic Shrink Wrapper

PSW-450
Industrial line

REF: tradeindia/5325717
01 — Conveyor

A belt that doesn't stutter

PU-coated belt over a fabricated MS frame, driven by a VFD-controlled geared motor. Speed is dialled in real time from the HMI, so packaging cadence stays locked to the upstream filler.

02 — Heat Tunnel

Even heat, shrink without scorch

Stainless inner chamber, 8 finned heaters, recirculating turbine fans. PID closed loop holds the chamber within ±2°C end-to-end so the film grips uniformly across every face of the pack.

03 — Control

Operator-first HMI

7-inch capacitive touch with recipe storage, fault diagnostics, and shift counters. Works in low light, dust, and 45°C plant heat — because that's where it lives.
